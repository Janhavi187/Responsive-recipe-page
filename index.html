Breakfast


Veg Upma

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup semolina (rava/suji)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp urad dal (optional)
  • 1-2 green chilies (chopped)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • Mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans)
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • Curry leaves
  • 2.5 cups water

Instructions:

  1. Dry roast semolina on medium flame till light golden. Keep aside.
  2. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, urad dal, curry leaves, green chilies, and ginger.
  3. Add chopped onion and saut√© until translucent.
  4. Add chopped vegetables and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Pour in water, add salt, and let it boil.
  6. Gradually add semolina while stirring to avoid lumps.
  7. Cover and cook for 3-4 minutes until fluffy.
  8. Garnish with coriander and lemon juice.

Chole Masala

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp chole masala
  • Salt, turmeric, chili powder

Instructions:

  1. Pressure cook chickpeas until soft.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds.
  3. Add onions and saut√© until golden.
  4. Add ginger garlic paste and cook till raw smell goes.
  5. Add tomato puree and all spices.
  6. Add boiled chickpeas and simmer for 10 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with rice or roti.

Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp butter + 1 tsp oil
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric, chili powder, garam masala
  • 2 tbsp cream
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat butter and oil in a pan, add onions and saut√©.
  2. Add ginger garlic paste and cook.
  3. Add tomato puree, cook till oil separates.
  4. Add spices and paneer cubes, cook for 5 mins.
  5. Stir in cream, simmer for 2 mins, and serve hot.
